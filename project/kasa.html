KASA Show 2021.

Northwestern KASA is a student-run organization that welcomes all undergraduate students sharing an interest in Korean culture, community, and issues.

While KASA primarily serves as a home away from home for students who identify with as Korean or Korean-American, we also focus on spreading awareness for all of our campus by holding events pertaining to Korean heritage and culture. We also strive to mobilize our members to continue engaging with their communities beyond their time at Northwestern.
